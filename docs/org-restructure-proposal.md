## What Changes

### Howard's Role Evolves
**Before:** Route all work, manage all agents, be the middleman
**After:** 
- Strategic advisor to Brian (not a layer)
- Manages the daily rhythm (Dawn Patrol, Evening Debrief, memory)
- Cross-team coordinator when squads need to collaborate
- Oversees research & strategy pipeline (Noa â†’ Kai)
- Handles Brian's calendar, scheduling, logistics

Howard is more like a White House Chief of Staff â€” controls the information flow, sets the agenda, coordinates across teams, but the department heads report to the President.

### Brian's Engagement
- **Daily standup:** Brief async check-in with all 5 leads (via Dawn Patrol)
- **Weekly 1:1s:** Howard synthesizes, but Brian sees each lead's update directly
- **Strategic decisions:** Brian hears pitches directly, not filtered through Howard
- **Escalations:** Any lead can escalate directly to Brian

### Communication Flow
- **Vertical:** Each lead manages their team autonomously
- **Horizontal:** Cross-squad collaboration goes through Howard (coordinator, not approver)
- **Up:** Leads surface decisions to Brian; Howard ensures nothing falls through cracks
- **Down:** Brian's priorities flow through Howard to all leads
