# Needs Vocabulary Quiz

## Instructions
For each scenario, identify:
1. The likely **feelings** present
2. The underlying **needs** (at least 2)

Ask Howard to run this quiz interactively: "Quiz me on needs"

---

## Quiz 1: Recognizing Needs

### Scenario 1
> Your partner comes home late without calling. You notice your jaw tightening.

<details>
<summary>Possible feelings</summary>
Anxious, worried, frustrated, hurt, scared
</details>

<details>
<summary>Likely needs</summary>
Safety, consideration, connection, predictability, reassurance, communication
</details>

## Quiz 2: Feelings vs. Evaluations

**Identify whether each is a true FEELING or an EVALUATION disguised as a feeling:**

1. "I feel abandoned" → ___
2. "I feel scared" → ___
3. "I feel like you don't care" → ___
4. "I feel hopeful" → ___
5. "I feel manipulated" → ___
6. "I feel sad" → ___
7. "I feel unimportant" → ___
8. "I feel curious" → ___

<details>
<summary>Answers</summary>

1. EVALUATION (implies someone abandoned you)
2. FEELING ✓
3. EVALUATION (this is a thought about the other person)
4. FEELING ✓
5. EVALUATION (implies someone manipulated you)
6. FEELING ✓
7. EVALUATION (implies someone made you unimportant)
8. FEELING ✓

</details>

## Quiz 3: Needs vs. Strategies

**For each statement, identify the NEED underneath the STRATEGY:**

1. "I need you to text me when you'll be late"
   - Strategy: Texting when late
   - Need: ___

2. "I need us to have dinner together every night"
   - Strategy: Daily dinners
   - Need: ___

3. "I need you to say 'I love you' more often"
   - Strategy: Verbal affirmations
   - Need: ___

<details>
<summary>Possible needs</summary>

1. Consideration, predictability, respect, information
2. Connection, togetherness, quality time, intimacy
3. Reassurance, love, acknowledgment, to matter

</details>

## Quiz 4: Translating Jackal to Giraffe

**Translate these judgments into feelings + needs:**

1. "You're so selfish!"
2. "Why can't you ever be on time?"
3. "You never listen to me!"

<details>
<summary>Possible translations</summary>

1. "When I see [specific action], I feel hurt/frustrated because I need consideration/mutuality."

2. "When you arrive after we agreed to meet, I feel frustrated/anxious because I value reliability/respect for time."

3. "When I'm talking and you look at your phone, I feel sad/frustrated because I need to be heard/connection."

</details>
